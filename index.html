About

A bit about me

I'm a computer science student with a growing obsession with cybersecurity. I'm interested in how systems work — and how they fail. Whether it's writing a tool, understanding a vulnerability, or setting up a lab environment, I learn best by doing.

Outside of coursework, I spend time on personal projects that let me go deeper than the classroom allows. I'm especially drawn to network security, ethical hacking, and secure software development.

I also document my journey as a student on Instagram — the learning process, the challenges, and the small wins. I started it because I wished more people had done the same when I was getting started.
